Welcome to the official fan site of Presley Chweneyagae, the award-winning South African actor best known for his role in "Tsotsi".

About Presley

Presley Chweneyagae is a South African actor born in 1984 in Soweto. He gained international fame for his leading role in the Oscar-winning film "Tsotsi". Since then, he has starred in numerous films and TV shows in South Africa.

Achievements

  • Lead actor in Oscar-winning film "Tsotsi" (2005)
  • Winner of Best Actor at the South African Film and Television Awards (SAFTA)
  • Starred in numerous local and international films
